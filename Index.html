Architecture

These are all representative of the culture of Spain. Spain's architecture ranges from prehistoric monuments in minorca in the Balearic Islands, all the way to the Romans ruins of Merida and Tarragona. Also, has Gaudi's intricate fabulist sculptures in Barcelona. Towards the end of the 15th century, and before influencing Latin America with its Colonial architecture, Spain itself experimented with Renaissance architecture. In the 19th century, there were two faces: the efforts to achieve a new language and bring improvements using iron and glass. By th 20th century, the arrival of Modernism in the academic area produced figures as as Gaudí.
